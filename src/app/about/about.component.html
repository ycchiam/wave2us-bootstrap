How Wave2Us helps travellers like you?

by Wave2Us team

ADVANTAGES

1: SAVE TIME

Save a lot of time by delegating your trip planning to our Aces.  No more late night research on places to go to!

2: SAFETY

We require all of our Aces to provide you a contact number, and to show you how to get a local SIM card. During emergencies, having someone you can call to will make a huge difference. Besides, you will also know which areas are safe and which are not. Travel at ease, knowing that the Ace will always be on your back.

3: SAVE MONEY

Local restaurants, hidden attractions to the locals, efficient way of transportation, best location for accommodation, or special events in the town -- all of these is only possible with the insider information from the Ace.

4. DISCOVER HIDDEN GEMS

Where is the best place to have that local dish? Probably at some over hyped, overpriced place if you search the internet. Ask your Ace, and you will be pleasantly surprised by all the hidden gems to be discovered. There are a lot of interesting places that are only known to the locals and it can be difficult to find such information in the internet.

5. LANGUAGE/CULTURE

Traveling to a place where the language is a barrier can be difficult. With the help of the Ace, this is no longer an issue! The Ace can also advise you on the cultural differences, so you can be more prepared.

6. FLEXIBILITY

Sometimes, things just don't go the way you expect. Weather changes, or traffic problems, for example will hinder your itinerary. Fortunately, the Ace will be able to handle this crisis for you.

7. TRUST

Not just anyone can be an Ace, we only invite those we deem trustworthy. We take your safety very seriously, and we don't want you to have a bad travelling experience.

INVITE ONLY POLICY

As travelers in a foreign land, we want to know that we are in good hands. There is no way of knowing if the guide is an honest person or harbors malicious intent, without knowing the person before. That is why we believe that an Invite Only policy will help to create a better and safer traveling experience, for you and also for the Ace.

Our plan is to branch out from our own friends and family, and then form a community of trusted and honest Aces & Enjoyers to provide a safe and secure traveling experience. 

Do you know any of the Aces? Then join us!

PAYMENT

For now, we are accepting bank transfers or PayPal. Be assured, as you know who we are, and we know you as well (invite only, remember?). We guarantee that you will have all the service (accommodation, transport...) as planned.
